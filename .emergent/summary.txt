<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
Build a long-form, scroll-driven, cinematic vertical experience called How Deep Is the Ocean… Really?. This must be a premium, immersive, scroll-based web experience that takes 5–7 minutes to complete. The user scrolls downward through the ocean from surface level to the Mariana Trench, passing marine life, human achievements, and mind-blowing pressure comparisons.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
-   **Graphic Style:** Clean, minimal, semi-realistic marine illustrations with a deep ocean gradient background that darkens with depth.
-   **Structure:** A 10-section guided descent from the surface (0m) to the Mariana Trench (10,984m), with specific creatures, stats, and comparisons at each depth milestone.
-   **Interactive Elements:** Hover-over facts for species, live depth counter, smooth parallax scrolling, and animated marine life.
-   **Pacing:** A calm, 5-7 minute experience with ample breathing room between sections.
-   **Tech:** Must be responsive, performant, and use parallax scroll without page reloads.
-   **Emotional Goal:** To make the user feel small, curious, amazed, and thoughtful.
-   **Ending:** A conclusive ending section with a call-to-action button Explore Another Wonder.

**User's preferred language**: English
